DRDO

The DRDO was established in 1958 by amalgamating the Defence Science Organisation and some of the technical development establishments. A separate Department of Defence Research and Development was formed in 1980 which later on administered DRDO and its 50 laboratories/establishments. Most of the time the Defence Research Development Organisation was treated as if it was a vendor and the Army Headquarters or the Air Headquarters were the customers. Because the Army and the Air Force themselves did not have any design or construction responsibility, they tended to treat the designer or Indian industry at par with their corresponding designer in the world market. If they could get a MiG-21 from the world market, they wanted a MiG-21 from DRDO.

Poster Design

More than 10 jury members go through a primary selectingprocess. 5 special jury select the final winners. Equal prize amount will be provided for both digital and paperposter design winners. Special mentions will also be given at the valedictory ceremony. Creator’s Board is a poster designing competition. Participants will be provided a platform to flex their creative chops and design a poster for a topic given on the spot. Participants will be given 3 hours to complete their poster and submit it for assessment. They’ll also be required to record a video of themselves explaining their work and submit it along with their poster. The event has two subcategories, one category is for hand-drawn, on paper artwork and the other category is for computer-drawn, digital artwork. Participants who are submitting their work under the second category must also mention the software application they are using to create their poster. Posters will be assessed for their novelty, artistic flair, intent and explanation.
